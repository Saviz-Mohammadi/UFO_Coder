About me

Hi there! I’m Saviz Mohammadi, also known in the tech world as UFO Coder. I’m currently a computer science student at Simon Fraser University (SFU), where I’m diving deep into the fascinating world of technology. My focus areas include C/C++, Qt/QML, and CMake, and I’m always eager to tackle new challenges and expand my knowledge. My journey in coding has been a thrilling one, marked by hands-on experience with a variety of technologies.

I thrive on solving complex problems and turning ideas into functional, efficient solutions. Whether I’m working on a new project or experimenting with different tech, my passion for coding drives me to continuously learn and innovate. Thanks for stopping by my corner of the web. I’m excited to connect with fellow tech enthusiasts and explore new opportunities in the ever-evolving tech landscape! Feel free to explore some of my projects and courses listed below.
